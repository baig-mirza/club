Our Mission

Our mission is to unite the Australian-Indian community and encourage
self-improvement activities that help us grow as a community.

FOUNDERS

Men who made Auburn Brothers Club possible.

Naveed Mohammed

Naveed Mohammed is the president and also one of the main people behind this amazing organisation. He has successfully organised many events for the Indian sub-continent migrants settled in Auburn and other suburbs of NSW.

Imran Khan

Imran Khan, the vice president of the Auburn Brothers Club, has been instrumental in assisting numerous Indian immigrants who have established themselves in NSW.

Muzammil Siddiqui

Muzammil Siddiqui is an excellent secretary for the Auburn Brothers Club and has consistently gone above and beyond to help the migrants from the Indian subcontinent who have settled in various regions of New South Wales.
